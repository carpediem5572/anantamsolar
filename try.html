Technical Scope

01

Nano-Brush Wet Cleaning

Utilizing high-grade demineralized water and specialized solar brushes to eliminate hard water scaling and industrial soot without scratching the glass surface.

02

Thermal & Health Audit

Visual inspection for hotspots, cell browning, and micro-cracks that cause internal resistance and drop in power generation.

03

DC Integrity Management

Checking MC4 connector tightness and DC cable routing to mitigate potential fire risks due to loose connections.

04

Structural Tightening

Audit of module mounting clamps and earthing strips to ensure the plant is structurally secure against high wind loads.
