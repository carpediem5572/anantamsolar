Scope of Maintenance

Anantam Solar provides end-to-end solar asset management to ensure your investment delivers maximum returns. Our service goes beyond simple washing.

Professional Cleaning

Removal of bird droppings, industrial soot, and hard water scaling using specialized soft-bristle equipment and neutral agents.

Visual Inspection

Detailed check for micro-cracks, hotspots, and glass discoloration that might impact performance or safety.

Electrical Health Check

Verification of MC4 connectors, string health, and checking for any loose wiring or rodent damage in DC lines.

Structure Integrity

Periodic check of nut-bolts, clamps, and earthing strips to ensure the solar plant withstands high wind speeds.
